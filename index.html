    What is Hypertrophy?

    Hypertrophy is a new social media community and web application that allows gym-goers to securely store their workouts on the cloud in real-time, and provides users with the option to share their workouts with family and friends.

    Best of all, Hypertrophy allows users to access all of their workouts on any device!

    Real-time Cloud Storage

    Your workouts are stored on databases in the cloud and accessible at anytime from any device!

    Privacy

    You are in control of your data - all your data is automatically kept private. You decide what to share.

    Community

    Set a new personal record? Proud of a workout? You can choose to share your results with family and friends!
